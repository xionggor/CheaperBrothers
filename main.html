About

The Studio

Our journey in the signage industry began in 2019, shortly after our CFO and founder, Daniel Wu, earned his Bachelor's degree. Fast forward to 2024, we made the strategic decision to close our traditional physical printing shop on Canal St. and transition our entire operation online. By cutting out heavy overhead, we engineered a business model built entirely around bringing wholesale savings directly to our customers.

We partner closely with our primary manufacturer, bufanflex.com, to provide premium wholesale banner materials alongside our rigorous in-house installations. We offer a true end-to-end service—from receiving your file to mounting it flawlessly on your wall or vehicle. We believe in "no cheapest, only cheaper." If you receive a lower verifiable quote elsewhere, we offer a strict price match guarantee on our printing fees.
